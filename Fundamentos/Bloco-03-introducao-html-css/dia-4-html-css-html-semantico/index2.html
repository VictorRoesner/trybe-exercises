Descrição

O Stomatopoda (Odontodactylus scyllarus) é caracterizado por ser extremamente agressivo, presente há 400 milhões de anos atrás.

Unlike most crustaceans, the mantis shrimp is a ruthless killer. Even if they miss, the shockwave will kill its prey. The mantis uses this technique to bash its prey to pieces or spear them with their barbed tips. Its limbs strike so fast that the water around them boils and can light up from the heat.
